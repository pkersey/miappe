Projects

MIAPPE was originally developed in the context of the transPLANT project, which was funded by the European Commission within its 7th Framework Programme under the thematic area "Infrastructures", in collaboration with the European Plant Phenotyping Network (EPPN). It is currently being applied in the context of the ELIXIR-EXCELERATE. Furthermore within the EU Horizon 2020 call the project AGENT, INCREASE and CAPITALISE apply the MIAPPE aas well.
